RULES FOR INNOVATION PITCH

Get ready to showcase your research and presentation skills! Each team will have 10 minutes—8 minutes for presenting and 2 minutes for review. The event welcomes innovative ideas across various themes, including Cybersecurity, AI, Blockchain, Emerging Technologies, Sustainability, Space Tech, and Healthcare.
Rules:
✔ Team Size: 1-3 members
✔ Paper Format: IEEE or standard format (5-7 pages)
✔ Plagiarism Limit: Max 20%
✔ Judging Criteria: Content, Presentation, Design, Knowledge, and Innovation
✔ Disqualification: Exceeding time, plagiarism, or inappropriate content
